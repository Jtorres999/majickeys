So now that you have booked a trip to Disney (or are still in the planning stages) let me tell you about the Disney World Parks (Orlando, Florida location). Disney World has 4 theme parks. For each of these parks you must have a park ticket (109.00 plus tax and price varies depending on the day) and if visiting one day you must pay for a parking ticket (varies from $30-$55), unless staying at the resorts (free parking). Each park is centered around different Disney movie themes, characters, and rides. I will be describing each park below. So, buckle up and enjoy the ride.
